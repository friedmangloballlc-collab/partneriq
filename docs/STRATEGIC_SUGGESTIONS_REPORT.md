### SUGGESTION 2.6 — Add a "Fantasy League" Format as a Defined Pre-Season Marketing Activation

**How to implement:**

Season champion player wins a $10,000 DealStage brand deal fund credited to their platform account.
2. Launch timing: "Deal Draft" opens 2 weeks before the premiere, simultaneously with the contestant profile releases on the DealStage platform. This gives viewers a reason to research contestants before Episode 1 airs, driving early engagement and social discussion.
3. Distribution: partner with the show's streaming platform to integrate Deal Draft into their companion app if possible. If not, run it entirely through the DealStage platform. Promote it through every creator in the DealStage network (they are the natural audience) with a creator referral program where creators earn platform credits for every new Deal Draft player they refer.
