## 18.6 Objection Handling Library

### Price Objections

**Objection 1: "It's too expensive."**

Response: "I understand budget is a factor. Help me understand: too expensive compared to what? If you're comparing to free tools, you're right -- free is cheaper. But free tools cost you time: 8+ hours per week in manual work, missed partnerships from slow discovery, and deals lost because you're managing them in spreadsheets. At $299/month, if DealStage helps you close even one additional deal per month, what's that worth to your business?"

**Objection 2: "We can't justify the spend right now."**

Response: "When would the timing be better? If it's a budget cycle issue, our Free tier lets you start building your pipeline today at zero cost, and you can upgrade when budget opens up. If it's an ROI uncertainty, let me share data from customers in your space: [relevant metric]. Would a one-month pilot on Starter with a clear ROI target help you make the case internally?"

**Objection 3: "Your competitor is cheaper."**

Response: "Which competitor? [Pause.] If it's Upfluence, they're at $478/month -- our Starter is $299, so we're actually 37% less. If it's a different tool, let's compare what you're getting: DealStage includes AI matching, deal pipeline, media kits, brand intelligence, and a two-sided marketplace. What does the cheaper tool include?"

### Feature Objections

**Objection 4: "You don't have [specific feature]."**

Response: "Tell me more about how you'd use that feature. [Listen.] You're right, we don't have [feature] as a standalone module today. Here's how our customers handle that use case: [workaround or alternative]. Is that feature a dealbreaker, or is it a nice-to-have? If it's critical, I want to be upfront about that so you make the right decision."

**Objection 5: "We need Shopify/ecommerce integration."**

Response: "We integrate with ecommerce platforms through our API. For dedicated product seeding workflows -- shipping products to creators, tracking gifting -- our customers typically pair DealStage with their existing Shopify tools. The cost of DealStage plus a dedicated gifting tool is still less than Grin alone. What percentage of your campaigns involve product seeding vs. paid partnerships?"

**Objection 6: "AI matching sounds nice, but how accurate is it?"**

Response: "Fair question -- AI claims are everywhere and skepticism is healthy. Here's how to evaluate it: sign up for the free tier, run an AI match using your actual brand profile and target audience, and judge the results yourself. Our match scoring uses 5 dimensions: niche adjacency, tone matching, audience overlap, platform alignment, and engagement fit. Each match comes with a score breakdown so you can see exactly why the AI recommends each talent. Try it risk-free and let the results speak."

**Objection 7: "5 AI queries per month on the Free tier is too limiting."**

Response: "You're right -- 5 queries per month is designed for evaluation, not production use. It's enough to validate that our AI matching is relevant and accurate for your needs. Once you've seen the quality, Starter gives you 50 queries per month and Growth gives you unlimited. Think of the Free tier as a test drive, not the daily commute."

### Trust Objections

**Objection 8: "You're too new / not established enough."**

Response: "We have 1,200 brands and 44,847 contacts on the platform today. We're newer than Grin (2014) or CreatorIQ (2014), but that's also why our technology is modern -- we built on AI from day one rather than retrofitting it onto a decade-old architecture. We're not asking you to bet on us blindly: try the Free tier, see the results, and decide with data. Would you like to speak with a customer in your industry who can share their experience?"

**Objection 9: "What if you go out of business?"**

Response: "A fair concern with any vendor. A few things to consider: your data is always exportable -- you're never locked in. Our business is growing, our unit economics are strong, and our pricing model is sustainable (unlike platforms burning through VC funding with underpriced enterprise deals). I can share our customer retention metrics and growth trajectory if that helps. But the best insurance is that DealStage pays for itself in the first month -- the risk of trying us is near zero."

**Objection 10: "We need to see more case studies / social proof."**

Response: "Absolutely -- I'll send you case studies from customers in [their industry]. We have references from brands, agencies, and managers who can speak to specific use cases. I'd also recommend checking our G2 reviews for unfiltered feedback. Beyond that, the most convincing proof is your own experience: sign up for free, run your first AI search, and see the matches. Nothing I show you will be as convincing as results from your own brand profile."

### Process Objections

**Objection 11: "We need to involve IT / Security."**

Response: "Absolutely -- security review is important. I'll send our security documentation today: SOC 2 compliance status, data encryption approach, access controls, and data handling policies. If your IT team has a security questionnaire, send it over and we'll turn it around within 48 hours. What's the best way to connect with your IT point of contact?"

**Objection 12: "We're in the middle of [competitor] evaluation."**

Response: "No problem -- evaluating multiple options is smart. I'd suggest including DealStage in your evaluation so you have a complete picture. Our free tier lets you run a side-by-side comparison without any commitment. Most buyers who evaluate us alongside [competitor] find the AI matching quality and pricing advantage compelling. Can I set you up with a free account so you can include us in your comparison?"

**Objection 13: "We need to talk to our team first."**

Response: "Of course. Who on your team would be involved in the decision? I'd be happy to put together a one-pager tailored to their concerns -- whether that's technical architecture for IT, ROI projections for finance, or feature comparison for the marketing team. Would it be helpful to schedule a brief session with the key stakeholders next week?"

**Objection 14: "We'll think about it and get back to you."**

Response: "I appreciate that. To help frame your thinking: is there a specific concern that's holding you back, or is it more about finding the right time? If there's a concern, I'd rather address it now while we're both focused on it. If it's timing, let me suggest this: sign up for the free tier today so you can explore on your own schedule, and I'll check in [specific date] to see where you've landed. Does that work?"

**Objection 15: "We already have a solution that works well enough."**

Response: "'Well enough' is a fair bar. But let me ask: is 'well enough' costing you time you could spend on strategy? Is 'well enough' finding you the best talent, or just available talent? Is 'well enough' giving you AI-powered insights, or just a database to search through? The gap between 'well enough' and 'excellent' is usually where the biggest ROI hides. Let me show you what 'excellent' looks like in a 15-minute demo -- if the difference isn't significant, I'll tell you to stay with what you have."

## 21.2 Health Scoring Model

### Health Score Components

Each customer account receives a composite health score from 0-100, calculated weekly. The score is weighted by factors that predict retention and expansion.

| Component                | Weight | Measurement                                                                                                    | Scoring                                                                                              |
| ------------------------ | ------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Login Frequency**      | 20%    | Logins per week relative to expected frequency for their role and tier                                         | 100 = daily+; 75 = 3-4x/week; 50 = 1-2x/week; 25 = 1x/2 weeks; 0 = no login 14+ days                 |
| **AI Query Utilization** | 20%    | AI queries used as % of plan limit                                                                             | 100 = 80-100% utilized; 75 = 50-79%; 50 = 25-49%; 25 = 1-24%; 0 = 0% utilized                        |
| **Deal Activity**        | 20%    | Deals created, progressed, or completed in the period                                                          | 100 = 5+ active deals; 75 = 3-4 deals; 50 = 1-2 deals; 25 = deals exist but no movement; 0 = 0 deals |
| **Feature Breadth**      | 15%    | Number of distinct features used (match engine, pipeline, media kits, analytics, intelligence, contact finder) | 100 = 5+ features; 75 = 4 features; 50 = 3 features; 25 = 2 features; 0 = 1 or fewer                 |
| **Response Time**        | 10%    | Average response time to deal inquiries and match notifications                                                | 100 = <4 hours; 75 = 4-24 hours; 50 = 1-3 days; 25 = 3-7 days; 0 = >7 days or no response            |
| **Team Engagement**      | 10%    | For multi-seat accounts: % of seats active in the period                                                       | 100 = 90%+ active; 75 = 70-89%; 50 = 50-69%; 25 = 25-49%; 0 = <25%                                   |
| **NPS/Feedback**         | 5%     | Most recent NPS score or feedback sentiment                                                                    | 100 = NPS 9-10; 75 = NPS 7-8; 50 = NPS 5-6; 25 = NPS 3-4; 0 = NPS 0-2 or no response                 |

### Health Score Classification

| Score Range | Classification | Color       | Action                                                                           |
| ----------- | -------------- | ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 85-100      | **Healthy**    | Green       | Monitor, identify expansion opportunities, request testimonials                  |
| 70-84       | **Stable**     | Light Green | Standard engagement cadence, optimize feature adoption                           |
| 50-69       | **At Risk**    | Yellow      | Proactive outreach within 48 hours, usage review, offer training                 |
| 30-49       | **Unhealthy**  | Orange      | Urgent outreach within 24 hours, executive sponsor engagement, intervention plan |
| 0-29        | **Critical**   | Red         | Same-day outreach, escalation to CS leadership, retention offer if appropriate   |

### Health Score by Role — Expected Benchmarks

| Role    | Healthy Threshold | Typical Healthy Score | Key Risk Indicator                                              |
| ------- | ----------------- | --------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Talent  | 70+               | 78                    | Login frequency drop + 0 deal activity                          |
| Brand   | 75+               | 82                    | AI query utilization <25% + deal pipeline stagnant              |
| Agency  | 80+               | 85                    | Active client workspaces decreasing + team engagement declining |
| Manager | 75+               | 80                    | Roster size shrinking + deal activity declining                 |
